South China Tiger

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris amoyensis
  • Population: Possibly Extinct in Wild
  • Habitat: South-central China
  • Threats: Habitat loss, historical hunting

Sumatran Rhinoceros

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Dicerorhinus sumatrensis
  • Population: Less than 80
  • Habitat: Tropical rainforests of Indonesia
  • Threats: Poaching, habitat loss, small population size

Vaquita

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Phocoena sinus
  • Population: Less than 20
  • Habitat: Northern Gulf of California
  • Threats: Illegal fishing practices, bycatch

Chinese Pangolin

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Manis pentadactyla
  • Population: Unknown, Rapidly Declining
  • Habitat: Northern and southern China, Taiwan, Myanmar, Vietnam
  • Threats: Poaching for meat and scales, habitat loss

Saola

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Pseudoryx nghetinhensis
  • Population: Unknown, Extremely Rare
  • Habitat: Annamite Range of Laos and Vietnam
  • Threats: Hunting, habitat loss, snares set for other animals

Amur Leopard

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Panthera pardus orientalis
  • Population: Less than 100
  • Habitat: Temperate forests of Far Eastern Russia and China
  • Threats: Poaching, habitat loss, inbreeding

Javan Rhino

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Rhinoceros sondaicus
  • Population: Around 75
  • Habitat: Ujung Kulon National Park, Java, Indonesia
  • Threats: Poaching, small population size, natural disasters

Bornean Orangutan

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Pongo pygmaeus
  • Population: Around 104,700
  • Habitat: Lowland rainforests of Borneo
  • Threats: Deforestation for palm oil, hunting, forest fires

Gharial

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Gavialis gangeticus
  • Population: Less than 250 adults
  • Habitat: Rivers of the Indian subcontinent
  • Threats: Habitat loss, fishing nets, sand mining, dams
