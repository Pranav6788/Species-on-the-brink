Northern Hairy-nosed Wombat

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Lasiorhinus krefftii
  • Population: Less than 300
  • Habitat: Queensland's Epping Forest National Park
  • Threats: Habitat loss, predation, disease, low genetic diversity

Orange-bellied Parrot

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Neophema chrysogaster
  • Population: Less than 50 in wild
  • Habitat: Coastal areas of southern Australia, Tasmania
  • Threats: Habitat destruction, predation, climate change

Gilbert's Potoroo

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Potorous gilbertii
  • Population: Less than 100
  • Habitat: Two Islands Nature Reserve, Western Australia
  • Threats: Habitat loss, predation by foxes and cats, bushfires

Mountain Pygmy-possum

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Burramys parvus
  • Population: Less than 2000
  • Habitat: Alpine and sub-alpine areas of southeast Australia
  • Threats: Climate change, habitat loss, feral animals

Western Swamp Tortoise

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Pseudemydura umbrina
  • Population: About 200 individuals
  • Habitat: Swan Valley, Western Australia
  • Threats: Urban development, reduced rainfall, fires

Leadbeater's Possum

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Gymnobelideus leadbeateri
  • Population: Less than 1000
  • Habitat: Central Highlands of Victoria
  • Threats: Logging, bushfires, climate change
