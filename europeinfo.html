Iberian Lynx

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Lynx pardinus
  • Population: Around 1,100 (recovering)
  • Habitat: Mediterranean woodland in the Iberian Peninsula (Spain and Portugal)
  • Threats: Habitat fragmentation, decline of rabbit populations, road mortality

Saimaa Ringed Seal

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Pusa hispida saimensis
  • Population: Less than 400
  • Habitat: Lake Saimaa in Finland (only location)
  • Threats: Climate change affecting ice formation, fishing net entanglement, human disturbance

Mediterranean Monk Seal

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Monachus monachus
  • Population: 700-800
  • Habitat: Mediterranean Sea and Eastern Atlantic Ocean, particularly around Greece and Turkey
  • Threats: Habitat degradation, fishing interaction, pollution, human disturbance

European Mink

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Mustela lutreola
  • Population: Less than 5,000
  • Habitat: Rivers and wetlands in parts of Spain, France, Romania, and Russia
  • Threats: Competition from American mink, habitat loss, hunting, water pollution

Karpathos Marsh Frog

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Pelophylax cerigensis
  • Population: Unknown, very limited
  • Habitat: Only found on the island of Karpathos, Greece
  • Threats: Water extraction, drought, climate change, tourism development

Pyrenean Desman

Quick Facts

  • Scientific Name: Galemys pyrenaicus
  • Population: Declining rapidly
  • Habitat: Mountain streams in the Pyrenees (Spain, France, Portugal, Andorra)
  • Threats: Hydroelectric dams, water pollution, climate change, invasive species
